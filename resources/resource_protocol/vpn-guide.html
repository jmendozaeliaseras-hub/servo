VPN Guide

Recommended VPN providers for enhanced privacy

Why use a VPN?

  • Encrypt your internet traffic so your ISP cannot see what you browse
  • Hide your real IP address from websites and trackers
  • Access content while traveling or on restrictive networks
  • Protect your data on public Wi-Fi networks

ProtonVPN

Free tier — unlimited data Switzerland No-log policy

Operated by Proton AG (makers of ProtonMail). The free tier has no data cap but is limited to servers in 5 countries. Open-source apps, independently audited.

protonvpn.com

Windscribe

Free tier — 10 GB/month Canada No-log policy

Generous free tier with servers in 10+ countries. Includes a built-in ad blocker (R.O.B.E.R.T.). 10 GB monthly allowance, expandable by confirming email or tweeting.

windscribe.com

TunnelBear

Free tier — 2 GB/month Canada No-log policy

Easy-to-use VPN with a playful UI. Free tier is limited to 2 GB/month, suitable for light or occasional use. Annually audited by an independent security firm.

tunnelbear.com

Mullvad VPN

Paid — 5 EUR/month Sweden No-log policy

Gold standard for privacy. No email or personal info required to sign up — just a randomly generated account number. Accepts cash and cryptocurrency. Open-source, independently audited.

mullvad.net
